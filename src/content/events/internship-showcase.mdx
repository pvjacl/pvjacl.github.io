Join us in celebrating Equity in Historic Preservation Intern, Karino Wada, and her research, digital archive, and public history materials exploring Japanese American historic places in the White River Valley of Washington State.

The showcase will happen on Sunday, March 8, 2026 at 12:00pm to 2:00pm (Pacific Time) at the White River Buddhist Temple, 3625 Auburn Way N, Auburn, Washington. A presentation will also be available on Zoom.
