Obon (お盆) is the Japanese Buddhist holiday to honor the spirits of one's ancestors. It is of significant religious importance and is the biggest event of the year for the Temple. 

[White River Buddhist Temple](https://www.wrbt.org) will celebrate their annual Obon festival on June 25, 2026. More details should be announced at their website [Bon Odori page](https://www.wrbt.org/bonodori).
