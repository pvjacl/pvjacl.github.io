Gallery Ribbon Cutting

A soft opening with living survivors of the Puyallup Assembly Center were invited to cut the ribbon on Thursday, August 15, 2024.

Survivors pictured from left to right: Penny and Frank Fukui (also co-chairs of this project), Mary Abo, Choichi Shimizu, Elsie Taniguchi (former president of the this chapter) and Jim Yamaguchi (original architect)
